Pre-Trade Routine (5 Minutes)

  • Check your emotional state: Are you calm, anxious, excited, or tired? If not calm, don't trade.
  • Review your trading journal from yesterday.
  • Recite your risk limits: "I will not risk more than X% on any single trade today."
  • Set a maximum loss limit for the day (e.g., "If I lose 5% of my portfolio, I stop trading for 24 hours.")

During-Trade Discipline

  • Set price alerts instead of watching the chart every second.
  • Never check your portfolio value more than 3 times per day.
  • If a trade moves against you by 10%, don't "wait for it to come back." Reevaluate your thesis.
  • If a trade moves in your favor by 50%, take partial profits. Always.

Post-Trade Reflection (10 Minutes)

  • Log every trade in your journal immediately after closing.
  • Rate your emotional discipline on a scale of 1-10.
  • Note one thing you did well and one thing you can improve.
