📜 What Is the ARMA Bill? A Complete Overview

The American Reserve Modernization Act (ARMA) is the reintroduced and rebranded version of the earlier BITCOIN Act. After extensive revisions, the final draft takes a more measured approach than earlier iterations that proposed aggressive 1-million-token buying mandates.

🌍 Why the ARMA Bill Matters for Bitcoin & the Crypto Market

The ARMA bill represents the most significant federal cryptocurrency legislation in US history. Here's why it matters:

1. Massive Demand Shock for Bitcoin

If the Treasury begins purchasing 200,000 BTC annually, that represents approximately $16 billion in buying pressure per year at current prices. With Bitcoin's daily mining output at roughly 450 BTC, government purchases alone could absorb over 100% of newly mined Bitcoin. Basic supply-and-demand economics suggests this would exert significant upward price pressure.

2. Institutional Legitimacy

A formal US government Bitcoin reserve would signal to every institutional investor, pension fund, and sovereign wealth fund that Bitcoin is a legitimate reserve asset. This psychological shift alone could unlock trillions in institutional capital that has been waiting on the sidelines.

3. Regulatory Clarity

By codifying self-custody rights and establishing a federal framework for digital assets, the ARMA bill provides the regulatory clarity that the crypto industry has been demanding for years. This reduces uncertainty risk for both retail and institutional participants.

4. Global Race for Bitcoin Reserves

If the US establishes a Strategic Bitcoin Reserve, other nations will likely follow suit. Countries like El Salvador have already paved the way, but US adoption would trigger a global FOMO wave among sovereign nations competing for scarce Bitcoin supply.

🏨 The Political Landscape: Will ARMA Pass?

The ARMA bill faces an uncertain but increasingly favorable path through Congress:

  • Bipartisan Support: With 14+ co-sponsors from both parties, ARMA has broader political backing than any previous crypto legislation.
  • Trump Administration Backing: President Trump's pro-crypto executive orders have set the stage for this legislation. The administration has already signaled support for a Strategic Bitcoin Reserve. Read our Trump crypto executive order analysis for context.
  • Senate Companion Bill: Senators Bill Cassidy and Cynthia Lummis have introduced separate Senate legislation to formalize the Bitcoin reserve, suggesting coordinated effort across both chambers.
  • State-Level Momentum: At least two US states have already enacted their own strategic Bitcoin reserve laws, demonstrating grassroots support for the concept.

Our Assessment: Moderate-to-high probability of passage in some form within 12-18 months. The bipartisan framing, reduced buying mandate (compared to earlier versions), and self-custody protections make this one of the most politically viable crypto bills ever introduced.
