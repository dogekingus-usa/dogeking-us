Our Scoring Framework: What Makes a Meme Coin "Strong"

We evaluate each token on six weighted metrics:

  • Holder Count & Growth (25%) Total unique wallets holding the token, plus 30-day growth rate. Organic growth beats airdrop farming.
  • Liquidity Depth (20%) Total value locked in DEX pools and the depth of order books at 2% slippage. Thin liquidity = rug risk.
  • Volume-to-Market Cap Ratio (15%) Healthy meme coins trade between 15-40% of their market cap daily. Higher = more active ecosystem.
  • Holder Concentration (15%) Percentage of supply held by top 10 wallets. Under 15% suggests healthy distribution.
  • Developer Activity (15%) GitHub commits, smart contract upgrades, and audit frequency. Active devs = ongoing project.
  • Community Engagement (10%) Twitter/X followers, Discord activity, Telegram growth. Quality over bot-count.

On-Chain Red Flags: What We Screened Out

In preparing this analysis, we screened over 200 Solana meme coins and excluded tokens that exhibited these red flags:

  • Top 10 holder concentration above 25% Indicates whale manipulation risk. Four tokens with strong narratives were excluded on this metric alone.
  • Liquidity below $500K across all DEXs Too thin for meaningful position sizes. High slippage makes trading unprofitable.
  • Holder count declining for 30+ days Indicates net selling pressure and waning interest. Several formerly popular coins fit this pattern.
  • No smart contract updates in 6+ months Abandoned projects often have stale codebases and no future development roadmap.
  • Sudden supply changes Tokens where the total supply mysteriously increased (mint function still active). Immediate red flag.

How to Build a Meme Coin Watchlist

Use the five tokens above as your starting point, then supplement with your own research:

  1. Set up Birdeye alerts for each token's price, volume, and holder count changes.
  2. Join the official communities Discord and Telegram quality tells you more than any metric can.
  3. Track liquidity depth daily. A sudden drop in DEX liquidity often precedes a major price move (up or down).
  4. Monitor top wallet movements using Solscan. If the top 10 holders start selling, it's time to reduce your position.
  5. Keep a trading journal tracking your entries, exits, and the on-chain conditions that led to each decision.
