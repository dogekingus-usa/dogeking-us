What is DeFi Yield Farming?

Yield farming is the process of earning rewards by providing liquidity to decentralized finance protocols. On Solana, it's fast, low-cost, and accessible to beginners.

Getting Started

Step 1: Set up a Phantom or Solflare wallet.
Step 2: Fund your wallet with SOL (for gas fees) and the tokens you want to farm.
Step 3: Choose your protocol: Jupiter (best aggregator), Raydium (largest DEX), Orca (user-friendly), Kamino (automated strategies), Marginfi (lending).
