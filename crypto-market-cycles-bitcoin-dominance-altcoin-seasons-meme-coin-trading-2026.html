🔎 On-Chain Metrics for Cycle Timing

Price action can be manipulated. On-chain data cannot. Here are the essential on-chain metrics that confirm what phase of the cycle we are in.

1. MVRV Z-Score

The Market Value to Realized Value Z-Score compares market cap to realized cap (the average price at which coins were last moved). When MVRV Z-Score exceeds 7, the market is overvalued and near a top. When it drops below 0, the market is undervalued and near a bottom. In January 2026, MVRV Z-Score hit 6.2—close to the overvalued zone but not yet at the 7+ warning level.

2. SOPR (Spent Output Profit Ratio)

SOPR measures whether holders are selling at a profit or loss. SOPR above 1.1 indicates profit-taking by long-term holders—a distribution signal. SOPR below 0.95 indicates panic selling—accumulation signal. Watch for SOPR resetting to 1.0 (break-even) during a rally as a sign of healthy consolidation.
