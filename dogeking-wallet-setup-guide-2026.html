5. Step-by-Step Setup (Phantom Wallet)

For this guide, we'll use Phantom as the recommended wallet. Here's how to set it up in under 5 minutes:

Step 1: Install Phantom

  1. Visit phantom.app on your desktop browser
  2. Click "Download" and select your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Brave, or Edge)
  3. Add the extension to your browser

Step 2: Create a New Wallet

  1. Click the Phantom icon in your browser toolbar
  2. Select "Create a New Wallet"
  3. CRITICAL: Write down your 12-word secret recovery phrase on paper. Store it in a safe place. Never store it digitally.
  4. Confirm your seed phrase by selecting the words in order
  5. Set a strong password (minimum 8 characters)

⚠️ CRITICAL WARNING: Your seed phrase is the ONLY way to recover your wallet. If you lose it, your $DKING tokens are gone forever. Never share it with anyone. Never enter it into any website. DogeKing team members will NEVER ask for your seed phrase.

Step 3: Fund Your Wallet with SOL

  1. Copy your Phantom wallet address (starts with a Solana address like 7x...aBcD)
  2. Purchase SOL from a centralized exchange (Coinbase, Binance, Kraken) and withdraw to your Phantom address
  3. Or use Phantom's built-in on-ramp to buy SOL with a credit/debit card
  4. Keep at least 0.01 SOL for transaction fees (swaps, transfers, staking)

Step 4: Add $DKING Token to Your Wallet

  1. In Phantom, click "Manage Token List" or the search icon
  2. Search for "DogeKing" or paste the $DKING contract address: UnKPK9oomuhdJz7cZAbaBEB5p3NhNVb6oUwyVP4oGJR
  3. Toggle the token to display it in your wallet
  4. Your $DKING balance will now appear in your wallet

6. How to Receive $DKING in Your Wallet

Once your wallet is set up, receiving $DKING is straightforward:

  1. Open your Phantom wallet
  2. Click "Receive" or your wallet address to copy it
  3. Send this address to the person or exchange sending you $DKING
  4. Wait for the transaction to confirm on Solana (typically 2-5 seconds)
  5. Your $DKING balance will update automatically

💡 Tip: Always send a small test transaction first (like $1 worth) when receiving from a new source. This confirms the address is correct before transferring larger amounts.

7. Security Best Practices

Protecting your $DKING tokens requires following essential security practices:

  • Use a hardware wallet for large holdings — Ledger Nano X or S+ for $500+ of $DKING
  • Never share your seed phrase — Not with anyone, not even "support"
  • Beware of phishing sites — Always verify you're on the correct DEX or website URL
  • Revoke unnecessary DApp permissions — Use solscan.io/token-revoke to clean up
  • Enable auto-lock — Set Phantom to auto-lock after 1 minute of inactivity
  • Use a separate wallet for daily transactions — Keep bulk $DKING in a "cold" wallet
  • Verify contract addresses — Only interact with the official $DKING token contract

⚠️ Common Scams to Avoid:

  • Fake "airdrop" websites asking for wallet connection
  • DM's from "customer support" — official support is always public
  • "Wallet verification" scams asking for seed phrase
  • Fake Phantom wallet apps from unofficial sources
