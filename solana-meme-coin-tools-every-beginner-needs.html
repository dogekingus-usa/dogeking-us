Tool #3: Solscan — On-Chain Detective

What it does: Blockchain explorer for Solana

Why you need it: When a token looks too good to be true, Solscan reveals the truth. Check the token holder distribution. If one wallet holds more than 20% of the supply, the developer can dump on you at any time.

What to look for: A healthy meme coin has 5,000+ holders with the top 10 wallets holding less than 15% of supply combined. Concentrated supply = high risk of rug.
