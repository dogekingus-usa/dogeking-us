Community & Governance

The DogeKing community is the heart of the kingdom. Here's how you can participate in shaping the future of $DKING:

Community Treasury

5% of total supply ($50,000,000 $DKING) is allocated to the Community Treasury, controlled by a multi-signature wallet requiring 3-of-5 signatures from elected community representatives. Treasury funds are used for:

  • Marketing campaigns and influencer partnerships
  • Community airdrop events and reward programs
  • Development bounties for ecosystem contributors
  • Exchange listing fee support
  • Charitable donations (animal welfare, blockchain education)

Governance Timeline

Community governance will be phased in throughout 2026. During Q1-Q2, decisions are made by the core team with community input via Telegram/Discord polls. Starting Q3 (post-NFT launch), NFT holders receive weighted governance votes. Full DAO governance is targeted for Q4 2026.
