Crash Buying Strategy — How to Execute

A list of coins means nothing without a strategy. Here's how to buy this dip without getting wrecked:

The 3-3-3 Method

  • 3 tranches: Split your total buy-in into 3 equal parts
  • 3 intervals: Buy one tranche every 3-5 days
  • 3% rule: Never allocate more than 3% of your portfolio to any single position in one transaction

Dollar-Cost Averaging Schedule

Instead of trying to catch the exact bottom (nobody can), set up a DCA schedule on your exchange of choice. Buy a fixed dollar amount of your chosen coins every 48-72 hours. This smooths out volatility and ensures you capture the average bottom, not the emotional peak of panic selling.

Setting Limit Orders

Don't buy at market price during a crash — the spread widens and you'll overpay. Set limit orders at key support levels and let the market come to you. MEXC and Binance offer advanced order types that make this easy.
