The bounce from this level has historically triggered altcoin and meme coin rallies — and today is no exception. With Bitcoin stabilizing, traders rotated capital into higher-beta meme coins.

2. Solana Ecosystem Momentum

Solana (SOL) is up 2.8% today, providing a solid base for the SPL token ecosystem. With Solana's sub-second finality and near-zero fees, meme coin traders are finding it the most efficient chain for quick entries and exits. 3. End-of-Month / Start-of-Quarter Rebalancing

June 3 marks the start of a fresh trading week. Institutional and retail traders alike are rebalancing portfolios after May's choppy price action. Capital is flowing from stablecoins back into volatile assets as risk appetite increases. Meme coins, as the highest-beta crypto assets, benefit disproportionately from this capital rotation.

Is This the Start of a Sustained Rally?

To determine whether today's green wave is the start of a sustained uptrend or just a dead cat bounce, traders should watch these three signals:

1. BTC Must Hold $68K

Bitcoin reclaimed $68K during today's Asian session. For the meme coin rally to continue, BTC needs to close the daily candle above $68K and ideally above $68,500. A failure to hold $67.5K would invalidate the bounce and likely trigger a second wave of selling.

2. Total Meme Coin Market Cap

The total meme coin market cap needs to reclaim and hold above $85 billion. A daily close above this level would confirm the uptrend and open the door for a move toward $95-100 billion. A rejection at $85B would suggest we're still in a range-bound market.

3. Volume Trends

Sustained rallies require increasing volume. Today's volume is encouraging — BONK and DOGE are both trading at 2x+ their 20-day average. However, we need to see this volume sustain for at least 48 hours before calling it a confirmed trend reversal.
