Solana NFT Marketplaces Comparison 2026

Solana has the most active NFT ecosystem outside of Ethereum. Multiple marketplaces compete for traders, each with unique features. Here is how they compare.

Magic Eden

The largest Solana NFT marketplace with the most listings and highest trading volume. Features include launchpad for new projects, rarity tools, collection analytics, and cross-chain support for Ethereum and Polygon. Fees: 2% per transaction. Magic Eden is the go-to marketplace for most NFT traders due to its liquidity and features.

Tensor

The fastest-growing Solana marketplace focused on advanced traders. Features include portfolio tracking, automated bidding, sweep functionality (buy multiple NFTs at once), and real-time analytics. Tensor has gained significant market share by offering tools that power users need. Fees: 1.5% per transaction. Best for active traders who want professional tools.

Solsea

The original Solana NFT marketplace with a focus on creator tools and royalties. Features include minting tools for creators, royalty enforcement, and collection management. Fees: 2% per transaction. Best for creators launching new collections.

DogeKing

The meme coin royalty on Solana. Fast transactions, community-driven, built for the people.
