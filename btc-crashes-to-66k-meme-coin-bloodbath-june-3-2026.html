Why Is This Happening?

Multiple factors are converging to create this selloff:

1. Mt. Gox Repayment Fears

The Mt. Gox trustee is reportedly preparing to distribute ~$9 billion worth of Bitcoin to creditors starting this month. While these fears have been circulating for weeks, the market is now pricing in potential sell pressure from recipients who've waited over a decade for their funds.

2. Macro Headwinds

The Fed's June FOMC meeting is two weeks away (June 17-18), and the market is pricing in a 35% probability of a rate hike ” up from 20% just a week ago. Higher rates = less appetite for risk assets = crypto selloff.

3. Leverage Flush

Funding rates had been persistently positive for 10 consecutive days, indicating overcrowded long positions. The market was due for a leverage flush, and it's happening violently. The positive side: clean leverage means the next rally will have a healthier foundation.
