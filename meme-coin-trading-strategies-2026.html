Rule #3: Set Hard Stop-Losses Before You Enter

In meme coin trading, the market moves too fast for manual exits. By the time you see the red candle and decide to sell, you're already down 40%. You need automated stop-losses.

Stop-Loss Levels by Coin Type:

  • Blue-Chip Memes: 15-20% stop-loss. These are liquid enough to execute smoothly.
  • Mid-Cap Gems: 20-30% stop-loss. Wider range accounts for natural volatility.
  • Low-Cap Plays: 30-40% stop-loss. These coins can swing 50% in an hour.
  • Speculative Bids: 50% stop-loss or no stop at all (treat as full risk). Only allocate what you're willing to lose entirely.

Rule #4: Take Profits in Stages

Nobody has ever gone broke taking profits. Yet most traders don't do it because they're convinced the coin will go higher. News flash: even if it does, taking partial profits along the way locks in gains and removes the emotional weight from the trade.

The 25% Scaling Plan:

Profit-Taking Schedule:

  • +50% gain: Sell 25% of your position. Take your initial risk off the table.
  • +100% gain (2x): Sell another 25%. You've now recovered your entire initial capital plus profit.
  • +200% gain (3x): Sell another 25%. You're now playing with house money.
  • +400% gain (5x): Sell the remaining 25% or let it ride. At this point, every dollar is pure profit.

This approach means you never exit a trade with regret. If the coin goes to zero after your first sell, you've already recovered your cost basis. If it 100x's, you still captured significant upside on the remaining position.

Rule #5: Rotate Into Blue-Chip Memes After Runs

Here's a pattern that repeats every cycle: low-cap meme coins explode first, then the rotation moves to mid-caps, then finally to blue chips like DOGE and SHIB. When the blue chips start pumping hard, it usually signals the final leg of the alt season.

The rotation strategy is simple:

  • In early alt season, allocate heavily to low-cap and mid-cap plays (higher risk, higher reward).
  • As the season matures and you accumulate 2-5x gains, progressively rotate profits into blue-chip memes and stablecoins.
  • When DOGE starts running 20-30% in a single day, it's often the signal that the cycle is nearing its peak.
