What Are Meme Coins?

Meme coins are cryptocurrencies built around internet culture, jokes, and community movements. Unlike Bitcoin (digital gold) or Ethereum (smart contracts), meme coins don't need a technical whitepaper or product roadmap. Their value comes from a combination of community enthusiasm, virality, brand recognition, and speculative trading volume.

In 2026, meme coins on Solana represent over 40% of daily DEX volume on the network. The low transaction fees ($0.0002 per swap) and near-instant finality make Solana the dominant chain for meme coin trading.

Key Characteristics of Meme Coins

  • Community-driven: Value is largely determined by community size, engagement, and cultural relevance.
  • High volatility: 50-100% daily moves are normal. A coin can 10x in a week or drop 90% in a day.
  • Low barrier to entry: Anyone can create a meme coin in minutes on Pump.fun for less than $1.
  • Speculative nature: Most meme coins have no utility. They trade purely on hype, attention, and momentum.
  • Network effects: The most valuable meme coins have the largest, most active communities.
