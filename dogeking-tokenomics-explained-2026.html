Every $DKING transaction has a 1% burn fee. This 1% is automatically sent to a dead wallet address, permanently removing those tokens from circulation.

$DKING is available on Solana DEXs including Jupiter, Raydium, and Orca. You need a Solana wallet like Phantom or Solflare to trade.
