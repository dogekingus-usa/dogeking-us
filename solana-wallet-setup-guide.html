Step-by-Step Phantom Wallet Installation — Fully Illustrated

Before you can buy your first Solana meme coin or stake $DKING tokens, you need a Solana wallet. This guide walks you through setting up Phantom — the most popular and user-friendly Solana wallet with 3M+ active users. No prior crypto experience required.

1 Download Phantom Wallet

Phantom is available as a browser extension (Chrome, Brave, Firefox, Edge) and as a mobile app (iOS & Android).

  • Desktop: Go to https://phantom.app and click "Download for [your browser]"
  • Mobile: Search "Phantom" in the App Store or Google Play Store
  • Important: Only download from the official Phantom website or official app stores. There are many fake phishing wallets out there.
Phantom.app homepage with Download button highlighted

2 Create a New Wallet

After installing the extension, click the Phantom icon in your browser toolbar and select "Create a New Wallet".

  • You’ll be asked to create a password (at least 8 characters, use a strong unique password)
  • Phantom will generate your Secret Recovery Phrase (12 words) — this is the most critical step

3 Secure Your Recovery Phrase

Phantom will display your 12-word recovery phrase one word at a time. Follow these security best practices:

  • Write it down on paper — two copies stored in separate locations
  • Use a metal seed plate for fire/water protection (optional but recommended for large holdings)
  • Never store it in a digital file, cloud service, email, or note app
  • Never enter it into any website or app other than Phantom itself (for wallet recovery)
Phantom recovery phrase display with 12 blank word slots being filled

4 Verify Your Phrase

Phantom will ask you to confirm your recovery phrase by selecting the words in the correct order. This ensures you’ve written them down properly. Complete this step before proceeding.

Phantom phrase verification screen with word tiles to arrange

5 Set Up Your Wallet Profile

Once your phrase is verified, you can optionally:

  • Set a nickname for your wallet (useful if you manage multiple wallets)
  • Choose a display theme (Dark/Light mode)
  • Pin the Phantom extension to your browser toolbar for quick access

Your default Solana address will be automatically generated. It looks like: 7aB3...XyZ9

Phantom wallet dashboard showing balance, tokens, and activity

6 Add SOL to Your Wallet

To start trading, you need SOL (Solana’s native token) for transaction fees. Every Solana transaction costs a fraction of a cent in SOL.

  • Option A: Buy SOL directly inside Phantom using MoonPay or Simplex (credit/debit card, Apple Pay, Google Pay)
  • Option B: Transfer SOL from a centralized exchange like Coinbase, Binance, or Kraken

7 Connect to dApps (Decentralized Apps)

Now you can connect your Phantom wallet to Solana dApps like Jupiter (best swap aggregator), Raydium (DEX), or dogeking.us for staking and token purchases.

  • Visit any Solana dApp and click "Connect Wallet"
  • Select Phantom from the wallet list
  • Approve the connection request in the Phantom pop-up
  • You’re now connected! Always verify the website URL before approving any transaction

8 Buy Your First Meme Coin

Ready to buy $DKING or another Solana meme coin? Here’s the quickest method using Jupiter Exchange:

  • Go to jup.ag and connect your Phantom wallet
  • Select SOL as the source token and the meme coin as the target token
  • Enter the amount of SOL to swap
  • Review the price impact and slippage (2–5% recommended for meme coins)
  • Click "Swap" and approve the transaction in Phantom
  • The tokens will appear in your Phantom wallet automatically
