What Triggered the Selloff?

The overnight selloff appears to be a continuation of the capital rotation theme highlighted by K33 Research. As we noted in yesterday's analysis, institutional capital has been rotating out of range-bound bitcoin and into high-flying AI stocks ” and that rotation accelerated during the Asian session.

Several factors converged:

1. AI stock momentum sucking liquidity. The opportunity cost of holding BTC while AI equities rip higher is pushing institutional traders to rebalance. K33 called it a "choppy summer" for bitcoin, and today's price action confirms that outlook.

2. Futures liquidation cascade. Bitcoin's drop through the $69K support level triggered a cascade of long liquidations on Binance and Bybit, accelerating the move lower through low-liquidity overnight hours.

3. Macro uncertainty. Ongoing U.S. sanctions on Iranian crypto exchanges and general regulatory noise added a layer of risk-off sentiment to the overnight session.

Meme Coins Feel the Heat

Meme coins, which tend to amplify bitcoin's moves, saw steeper percentage losses. BONK led the decline at -7.2%, followed by PEPE at -6.6% and DOGE at -5.7%. $DKING showed relative strength against the broader selloff, declining roughly in line with SOL rather than amplifying it ” a sign that $DKING holders are longer-term focused rather than panic-selling.

The meme coin market cap has pulled back from recent highs, but this is a familiar pattern. During the June 2025 cycle, meme coins rebounded 30-50% within 72 hours of similar BTC-driven dips.

Dip-Buying Strategy for Meme Coins

For traders looking to deploy capital during this dip, here's a structured approach:

Wait for BTC to establish a floor. The $66K-$67K zone is the next major support. If BTC holds above $66K, the dip is likely a buying opportunity. A break below $65K would signal a deeper correction, and meme coin exposure should be reduced.

Scale in, don't go all-in. Split your intended position into 3 tranches. Deploy 1/3 at current levels, 1/3 if BTC tests $66K, and 1/3 on confirmation of a reversal (BTC reclaiming $69K).

Focus on quality tokens. This is not the time for micro-cap gambles. Stick to tokens with proven communities, active development, and exchange listings: $DKING, BONK, WIF, and established ecosystem plays.
