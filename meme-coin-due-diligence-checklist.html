>>>>>>> 3ff2d3c6913ee0da875243087e60a8af0e98a1a7

Every legitimate meme coin has its contract verified on Solscan. Never buy a coin you can't verify.

  • Contract verified? Check on Solscan.io or Birdeye. Verified = green checkmark next to contract.
  • Mint authority disabled? If mint authority is still active, devs can mint infinite tokens. This is the #1 red flag.
  • Freeze authority disabled? If active, devs can freeze your tokens. Only acceptable for regulated tokens.
  • LP tokens burned or locked? Check if liquidity pool tokens were burned (sent to dead wallet). If not locked, devs can pull liquidity at any time.
  • Top 10 holders % < 30%? High concentration = risk of dump. Anything above 50% is a major red flag.
