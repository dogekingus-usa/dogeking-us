Ethereum Staking (Post-Merge)

Since Ethereum's transition to proof-of-stake, staking has replaced mining as the primary validation method. You can stake 32 ETH to run a validator node (earning ~3.5% APY) or use liquid staking platforms like Lido and Rocket Pool with as little as 0.01 ETH. The key advantage: no hardware costs, no electricity bills.
