What You Need Before Buying

Before you buy $DKING, gather these three things:

  1. A Solana-compatible wallet — Phantom, Solflare, or Backpack (we recommend Phantom for beginners)
  2. SOL tokens — Solana's native currency, used to pay for transaction fees and as the trading pair for $DKING
  3. A DEX aggregator — Jupiter Exchange is the most reliable option on Solana

Total cost to get started: as little as $5–$10 for your first $DKING position plus ~$0.0002 in transaction fees.

Step 1: Install Phantom Wallet

Phantom is the most popular Solana wallet with over 3 million monthly active users. Here's how to set it up:

  1. Go to phantom.app
  2. Download the browser extension (Chrome, Brave, Firefox, or Edge) or the mobile app (iOS/Android)
  3. Click "Create New Wallet"
  4. WRITE DOWN YOUR SEED PHRASE ON PAPER. Never store it digitally, never share it with anyone. This is the only way to recover your wallet.
  5. Set a strong password
  6. Your wallet is ready. You'll see a Solana address starting with 0x or a short domain if you have a Solana Name Service (.sol) domain.
⚠ CRITICAL: No legitimate support team will ever ask for your seed phrase. If anyone asks — in DMs, Discord, Telegram, or email — they are a scammer. Report and block immediately.

Step 2: Buy and Deposit SOL

You need SOL to swap for $DKING and to pay transaction fees (typically less than $0.01 per transaction on Solana).

Option A: Buy SOL on a Centralized Exchange

  1. Sign up for a exchange like Coinbase, Binance, or Kraken (if you don't already have an account)
  2. Buy SOL with your preferred payment method (bank transfer, debit card, etc.)
  3. Copy your Phantom wallet address (click your wallet address in Phantom to copy)
  4. Withdraw SOL from the exchange to your Phantom address

Option B: Buy SOL Directly in Phantom

  1. Open Phantom wallet
  2. Click "Buy"
  3. Select your region and payment method
  4. Complete the purchase through Phantom's integrated on-ramp (MoonPay, Transak, or Banxa)
  5. SOL appears in your wallet within minutes

Minimum recommended: 0.1 SOL (~$10–15) to cover the purchase plus a buffer for fees.

Step 3: Connect to Jupiter Exchange

Jupiter Exchange is Solana's leading DEX aggregator. It finds the best price for your swap across all Solana DEXs including Raydium, Orca, Meteora, and more.

  1. Go to jup.ag
  2. Click "Connect Wallet" in the top right
  3. Select Phantom from the wallet list
  4. Approve the connection request in your Phantom wallet
  5. You're now connected — your SOL balance will appear on the swap interface

Step 4: Swap SOL for $DKING

  1. On the Jupiter swap interface, select SOL as the "Pay" token (top field)
  2. In the "Receive" field (bottom), search for $DKING or paste the $DKING contract address
  3. $DKING Contract Address: Coming soon — verify on official DogeKing channels
  4. Enter the amount of SOL you want to swap
  5. Jupiter will show you the best available rate, price impact, and fees
  6. Review the details carefully
  7. Click "Swap" and approve the transaction in your Phantom wallet
  8. Wait 5–30 seconds for the transaction to confirm on Solana
  9. Done! Your $DKING tokens will appear in your Phantom wallet
💡 Pro Tip: If your $DKING tokens don't appear automatically in Phantom, you may need to add the token manually. In Phantom, go to "Manage Token List" → "Add Token" and paste the $DKING contract address.

Step 5: Store and Secure Your Tokens

$DKING tokens are stored on the Solana blockchain, not "in" your wallet. Your wallet contains the private keys that let you access and transfer your tokens. Here's how to keep them safe:

For Small Amounts (Under $500)

  • Phantom or Solflare browser wallet is sufficient
  • Keep your browser and wallet extension updated
  • Never connect to unknown dApps

For Larger Amounts ($500+)

  • Use a hardware wallet like Ledger Nano S/X with Phantom or Solflare
  • Hardware wallets keep your private keys offline
  • Always verify transaction details on your hardware device before signing

Security Checklist

  • ✅ Seed phrase written on paper, stored in a safe place (not on your computer!)
  • ✅ Two-factor authentication on your email and exchange accounts
  • ✅ No screenshots of your seed phrase
  • ✅ Never shared your private keys or seed phrase with anyone
  • ✅ Revoked unnecessary dApp permissions via Revoke.Cash

Frequently Asked Questions

Currently, $DKING is only available on Solana decentralized exchanges like Jupiter, Raydium, and Orca. Centralized exchange listings may come in the future — follow official DogeKing announcements for updates.

What are the fees for buying $DKING?

Solana transaction fees are typically $0.0002–$0.001 per transaction. Jupiter charges a 0.1% platform fee for some routes. The SOL→$DKING swap itself has liquidity provider fees of approximately 0.3% (varies by DEX).

How long does the swap take?

Solana blocks are produced every 400ms. A typical swap confirms in 5–30 seconds — dramatically faster than Ethereum or BNB Chain.

All crypto investments carry risk. $DKING is a meme coin, meaning its price is driven by community and market sentiment rather than traditional fundamentals. Never invest more than you can afford to lose, always verify contract addresses, and do your own research.
