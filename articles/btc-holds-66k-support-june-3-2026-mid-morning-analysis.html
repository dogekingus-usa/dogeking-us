The Stabilization Picture

The market has found a temporary floor. After the cascade that took Bitcoin from $68K to $66,477 in the overnight session, buyers have stepped in to defend the $66.5K level. The bounce from the low is modest (+$110), but it's significant because it shows there is demand at these levels.

What's most telling is the lack of follow-through selling. When a market crashes to fresh lows and then stabilizes without further breakdown, it suggests the selling pressure has been exhausted ” at least temporarily. The $380M in long liquidations that triggered the cascade have been flushed out.
