BTC Breaks Below $68K: New Support Levels & Meme Coin Strategy (June 2 Evening Update)

Published: June 2, 2026 | Updated: 10:05 PM ICT | Category: Market Analysis

Key Takeaway: Bitcoin has broken below the critical $68,000 support level, now trading at $67,777 (-5.2% in 24 hours). This marks the first major support breach since the afternoon sell-off accelerated. Meme coins are feeling the pressure, but $DKING continues to show exceptional relative strength, declining only an estimated -1% compared to DOGE (-2.5%) and SOL (-3.0%).

What Just Happened: $68K Support Breached

At approximately 9:30 PM ICT, Bitcoin's price slipped below the $68,000 psychological support level that had held since the early evening. In the 30 minutes since, BTC has continued to trade lower, currently sitting at $67,777 — a level not seen since the intraday low earlier this week.

This breakdown is significant because $68K represented the first major demand zone on the downside. With that level now acting as resistance, traders must recalibrate their expectations for the overnight session.

  • Bitcoin (BTC): $67,777 (-5.2%) — Below $68K, accelerating sell-off
  • Solana (SOL): $77.12 (-3.0%) — Following BTC lower
  • Dogecoin (DOGE): $0.0960 (-2.5%) — Modest decline relative to BTC
  • $DKING (est.): ~$0.00000000333 (-1%) — Remarkable relative strength

New Support Levels to Watch

With $68K now behind us, the following support levels come into focus for the overnight Asian session:

Level 1: $66,500 — The First Real Test

$66,500 represents the next significant demand zone. This level has acted as both support and resistance on multiple occasions over the past two weeks. If BTC can hold here, it would suggest the sell-off is running out of steam. A bounce from $66.5K would likely see a retest of $68K as resistance within 12-24 hours.

Level 2: $65,000 — The Psychological Floor

The $65,000 level is both a psychological round number and a technical support zone from mid-May. This is the line in the sand for bulls. A break below $65K would signal a more serious correction and likely trigger stop-loss cascades across the broader market, including meme coins.

Level 3: $63,500 — The Bear Case

If both $66.5K and $65K fail, $63,500 becomes the final support before a potential move toward $60K. At this point, we'd be looking at a -10%+ correction from today's highs, which would constitute a significant market event.

How This Affects Meme Coins

The evening sell-off has created a tiered impact across the meme coin market:

$DKING — The Relative Strength Leader

Despite BTC's -5.2% plunger, $DKING is holding remarkably well with an estimated decline of only -1.0%. This outperformance is consistent with the pattern we've observed all day. When BTC dropped from $69K to $68K earlier this evening, $DKING barely budged. Now with BTC below $68K, $DKING continues to find buyers.

This relative strength suggests one of two things: either $DKING has genuinely decoupled from BTC in the short term due to its own organic demand, or we're seeing accumulation by smart money ahead of a catalyst. Either scenario is bullish for $DKING holders.

DOGE (-2.5%) and SOL (-3.0%) — Following BTC Down

Both Dogecoin and Solana are tracking BTC's decline more closely. DOGE at $0.0960 (-2.5%) and SOL at $77.12 (-3.0%) are experiencing typical beta-driven sell-offs. These moves are proportionate to BTC's decline and don't indicate any project-specific weakness.

1. Shift to Defense Mode

The $68K breakdown changes the risk profile. Consider reducing position sizes by 25-50% for overnight holds. The Asian session can be thin, and if BTC continues to slide toward $66.5K, meme coins could see a delayed reaction gap-down at the open.

2. Set Stops at $66.5K BTC

If you're holding meme coin positions overnight, set alert triggers at BTC $66,500. If that level breaks, be prepared to exit or hedge immediately. A break below $66.5K would likely trigger a wave of liquidations that would sweep through all altcoins.

3. Prepare Buy Orders at Lower Levels

For traders with cash reserves, a BTC drop toward $65,000 would be a high-probability buy zone. Historically, meme coins with strong relative outperformers like $DKING tend to lead the bounce when BTC stabilizes. Consider setting limit orders at -15% to -20% from current prices for quality meme coins.

The Asian morning session (approximately 5:00 AM - 9:00 AM ICT) often sees reversals when U.S. evening sell-offs exhaust themselves. If BTC stabilizes overnight and holds $66.5K, expect a morning relief rally that $DKING could lead with 2-3x the bounce percentage of larger caps.
