6. Risks and Red Flags You Need to Know

Perp DEXs are not without risks. Here are the ones that matter most for meme coin traders.

6.1 Smart Contract Risk

Hyperliquid has not been significantly exploited to date, but every DeFi protocol carries smart contract risk. The HLP vault (which acts as the counter-party to trades) is an audited but still novel mechanism. Never trade more than you can afford to lose.

6.2 Funding Rate Risk

Meme coin perp markets on Hyperliquid can have extreme funding rates ” sometimes exceeding 1-2% per 8 hours when retail sentiment is overheated. If you're holding a perp position in a high-funding environment, your position can drain to zero from funding payments alone, even if the price doesn't move against you.

6.3 Liquidation Cascades

Because Hyperliquid's order book is thinner than Binance's for most pairs, a sudden price move can trigger cascading liquidations that send prices far beyond what fundamentals would suggest. This is both a risk and an opportunity ” but you need to size your positions accordingly.

6.4 Regulatory Uncertainty

Perp DEXs operate in a regulatory gray area in most jurisdictions. The CFTC has not taken enforcement action against Hyperliquid specifically, but the regulatory landscape for decentralized derivatives is still evolving. U.S. traders should consult with legal counsel before using unregulated perp platforms.
