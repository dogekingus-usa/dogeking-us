How to Use Jupiter DEX Aggregator on Solana

Published: May 16, 2026 | Updated: May 19, 2026 | 8 min read | Category: Trading

Jupiter is the single most important DeFi application on Solana. As a DEX aggregator, it finds the best swap routes across all Solana exchanges - Raydium, Orca, Meteora, and more - saving you money on every trade. In this guide, we'll cover everything from basic swaps to advanced features like limit orders, DCA, and cross-chain bridging.

Getting Started with Jupiter

Visit jup.ag and connect your Phantom or Solflare wallet. The interface is clean and intuitive. You'll see swap fields for "You Pay" and "You Receive" - simply select your tokens and enter an amount. Jupiter automatically routes through the best liquidity sources.

Basic Swaps

For a basic token swap, select the input and output tokens, enter the amount, and click "Swap." Jupiter will show you the route breakdown - which DEXs are being used and the price impact. Always check the slippage tolerance (default 0.5%) and adjust for volatile meme coins (1-5%).

Limit Orders

Jupiter Limit Orders allow you to set a target price for your trade. Your order executes automatically when the market reaches your price. This is perfect for buying the dip or taking profits without watching charts all day. Orders are executed through Jupiter's solana-based limit order system.

Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA)

Jupiter DCA lets you automate recurring purchases. Set up a weekly buy of SOL, USDC, or any token - Jupiter splits your order into smaller trades over time to minimize price impact. DCA is the most effective strategy for long-term accumulation.

Cross-Chain Swaps

Jupiter supports swaps between Solana and Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, and more through partnerships with bridging protocols like deBridge and Wormhole. This eliminates the need for manual bridging, though cross-chain swaps carry higher fees and longer settlement times.

Advanced Features

Price Impact Protection: Jupiter warns you when a trade would cause significant price impact. Slippage Configuration: Customize slippage per trade. Route Comparison: View all available routes ranked by expected output. Transaction Simulation: Preview your trade before signing.
