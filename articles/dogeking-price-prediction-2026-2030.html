Burn Mechanism Impact on Price

The 1% burn per transaction is $DKING's secret weapon for long-term price appreciation. Here's how it compounds over time:

  • Year 1: ~3-7% of supply burned (assuming moderate trading volume)
  • Year 3: ~10-20% of supply removed from circulation
  • Year 5: ~15-30% potential supply reduction

Unlike Dogecoin (which has 5B new coins mined annually with no cap), $DKING's deflationary mechanism creates increasing scarcity over time. This is similar to Shiba Inu's burn mechanism, which removed 41% of SHIB's initial supply within three years through strategic burns and transaction fees.
