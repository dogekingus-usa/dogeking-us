Solana continues to be the chain of choice for meme coin activity. With transaction fees under $0.01 and confirmation times under a second, Solana is where new money enters the meme coin market. $DKING, as one of Solana's most recognizable meme coin brands, directly benefits from every wave of new Solana users.

What This Rally Means for Summer 2026

A strong start to June historically predicts a strong summer for meme coins. Here's what the early June 2 data suggests:

  • Scenario 1 (Bullish): The rally extends through June and into July, with $DKING leading Solana meme coins to new all-time highs. DOGE and BONK follow, and the total meme coin market cap exceeds $150 billion by August.
