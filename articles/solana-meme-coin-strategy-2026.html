Why Solana in 2026?

Solana has emerged as the dominant blockchain for meme coins. Low fees ($0.0002/tx), high speed (65K TPS), and a massive developer ecosystem make it the natural home for community-driven tokens.

Portfolio Allocation Strategy

40% Blue Chips: $BONK, $WIF, $POPCAT - established projects with liquidity and community.
30% Mid-Cap Gems: Projects with $10-100M market cap, active development, growing community.
20% Early-Stage: New projects (under $10M) with strong fundamentals and low market cap.
10% Ultra-Speculative: Micro-cap plays with 100x potential.

Entry Strategy

Don't buy all at once. Use a 3-tranche system: 50% initial entry, 30% on first dip (-20%), 20% on second dip (-40%). Set limit orders to automate this.

Exit Strategy

At 2x: Sell your initial investment. You're now playing with house money.
At 5x: Take 50% profits. Let the rest ride.
At 10x: Take another 30%. Keep 20% for moon potential.
Set stop-losses: At -30% from entry for each position.

Research Framework

Before buying any coin, verify: Locked liquidity (LP locked 6+ months), Active Telegram/Discord (5K+ real members), Audited contract, Transparent team, Real utility or roadmap, Growing holder count.
