🏷️ The Burn Mechanism: How Supply Reduction Works

SHIB's primary path to higher prices is supply reduction through token burns. The Shiba Inu ecosystem has multiple burn mechanisms active in 2026:

Shibarium Gas Fee Burns

Since the Shibarium Layer-2 launch, a portion of gas fees (70% of base fees) is automatically converted to SHIB and burned. As of June 2026, Shibarium has processed over 450 million transactions since launch, with cumulative burns exceeding 180 billion SHIB.

ShibaSwap 2.0 Burn Rewards

The upgraded ShibaSwap DEX includes burn incentives for liquidity providers. A portion of trading fees is used to buy back and burn SHIB tokens directly from the open market. In May 2026 alone, this mechanism burned 4.2 billion SHIB.

Community-Led Burn Campaigns

The ShibArmy continues to organize voluntary burn campaigns through platforms like ShibaBurn and community-funded initiatives. While smaller in scale, these campaigns contribute to the overall burn narrative and community engagement.
