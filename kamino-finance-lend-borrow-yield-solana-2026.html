What wallet works with Kamino?

Kamino supports Phantom, Backpack, Solflare, and WalletConnect-compatible wallets. For the best experience, use Phantom or Backpack on desktop or mobile.

How much can I earn lending on Kamino?

Lending APYs vary by token and utilization. Stablecoins (USDC/USDT) typically earn 7-10% APY, while SOL earns 3-6% APY. Check live rates on the Kamino app.

What's the difference between Kamino and Marginfi?

Both are lending protocols on Solana, but Kamino offers additional products like Multiply (leveraged positions) and automated vaults. Marginfi focuses on a simpler lending/borrowing experience with lower fees. For a full comparison, see our Solana DeFi Deep Dive.

Can I use Kamino on mobile?

Yes! Kamino's web app works on mobile browsers. For the best mobile experience, use Phantom or Backpack's in-app browser to access app.kamino.finance.

What happens if Kamino gets exploited?

Kamino doesn't have an official insurance fund, but the team has been proactive about compensating users during minor incidents. For additional protection, consider diversifying across multiple lending protocols.
